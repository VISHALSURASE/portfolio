Voting Machine

Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) is a sophisticated electronic device used for voting in elections. It has revolutionized the voting process by replacing traditional paper- based voting methods with a more efficient, accurate, and secure system. EVMs have gained popularity worldwide due to their ability to streamline the electoral process, reduce human errors, and ensure the integrity of the voting system.

      EVMs are designed to be user-friendly and easy to operate, making them accessible to all voters, including those with disabilities. They typically consist of a control unit and a balloting unit connected together. The control unit is operated by the election officer, while the balloting unit is used by the voter to cast their vote.
